My name is Thomas Lars Nielsen. One of my greatest passions is music. This website is about me and my music. When I was young I played the violin. Since then I have learned to play the acoustic guitar, electric guitar, bass guitar and I sing. In high school I became really good friends with someone who has the same taste in music as me. Our senior year we began to go to concerts and to play guitar together. Now going to concerts and playing music with people have become some of my favorite things to do. Below you will find a list of some songs I have learned as well as pictures from concerts I have attended and a video of my first band. I recently have been learning more about music theory and scale work. At the bottom of the page is a link to the website of the band I recently joined: "Peaked in High School"
